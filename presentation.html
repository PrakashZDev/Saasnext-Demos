Optimization & Iterative Improvement Framework (The Feedback Loop)

The campaign is not static; it is a continuously evolving system driven by performance data.

  1. Phase 1: Launch & Baseline (Week 1): Launch 5 segments (e.g., Mumbai, Bangalore, Pune, etc.) with 3 creatives each.
  2. Phase 2: A/B Testing & Analysis (Week 2): Identify the top 2 performing cities and the best 1 creative in each.
  3. Phase 3: Budget Reallocation (Week 3): Shift 60% of the budget towards the top performers. Pause underperforming segments.
  4. Phase 4: Expansion & Scaling (Ongoing): Introduce new Tier-2 cities or new creative variants based on proven winners.

This ensures we fail fast on low performers and scale aggressively on high-ROI segments.
